Mission

"High educational aspiration for the right talent"

The trust is committed to improving the life chances of children and young people through excellence in education. We encourage the right talent one's to hold high aspiration in the academic learning. The purpose of the trust is to support and to Create a platform for extremely talented but economically challenged children and young people to pursue their education through koushal kranthi charitable trust. We believe that education is the most powerful weapon to change the world and make it a better place. Koushal kranthi charitable trust supports the talented children and young people through excellence in education as it’s a right, not a privilege. We help talented but economically challenged children to pursue their dreams in creating their career. The koushal kranthi charitable trust is established from a fundamental truth that every talented has a right for education. This very thought led to the development of our goals in raising global awareness of the challenges children and young people face in accessing education in today's world.
